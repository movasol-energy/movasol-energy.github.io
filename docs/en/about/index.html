Our Story

Founded in 2023 to establish market leadership in solar energy utilization, we began with regional project management and installations. Today, we exceed 200 MW of installed capacity and deliver nationwide construction and maintenance services for solar parks.

  • Comprehensive project management from site selection to grid connection
  • Real-time operational monitoring and predictive maintenance services
  • Innovative financing models and industry partnerships for sustainable growth

Our strategic objective is to lead technological advancements and efficiency improvements over the next decade. We are committed to carbon neutrality, reliability, and long-term value creation to ensure our clients' complete energy security.

Mission statement

Our mission is to advance a sustainable future through renewable energy solutions. Specializing in the design, construction, and long-term operation of solar parks, we provide our partners with reliable, cost-effective, and environmentally responsible power supply.

Planning & Construction

We manage end-to-end projects from initial site assessment through final commissioning, ensuring optimal system performance through technical expertise.

Maintenance & Operations

Through continuous monitoring and preventive maintenance, we maximize system lifespan and energy yield while minimizing downtime.

Sustainable Solutions

We deploy eco-friendly technologies designed for long-term economic and environmental responsibility.

ROI & Investment Protection

Leveraging cutting-edge technology, we secure stable, predictable returns for our stakeholders over the long term.

Certifications

Committed to the highest quality and safety standards, our certifications demonstrate our expertise and reliability in solar park design, construction, and maintenance.

ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management

Established and continuously improved quality management processes ensuring on-time project delivery and technical compliance.

ISO 14001 Environmental Management

Ongoing monitoring and reduction of environmental impact; eco-friendly execution and operations of green energy projects.

IEC 61215 PV Module Certification

Use of internationally standardized photovoltaic modules ensuring long-term performance and reliability.

Hungarian Energy Office License

Official operating license and grid connection authorization for high-capacity solar park installation and operation.

Our Team

Our team consists of highly skilled engineers, project managers, and technical experts with years of experience in the design, construction, and operation of solar parks. Charismatic leadership and dedicated professionals guarantee precise execution and continuous innovation.
