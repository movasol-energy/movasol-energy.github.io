Our Story

In 2023, we established MOVASOL ENERGY Plc. with the clear objective of leveraging our decades of experience in the Hungarian energy sector within a new, dynamic, and customer‑centric enterprise. Our founders, László Módos (formerly Deputy CEO of MAVÍR Plc.) and Gusztáv Varga (former senior executive at MVM OVIT Plc. and KOM Plc.), recognized the market’s need for a reliable partner that combines top‑tier expertise with agility and innovation. We became that partner.

Proven Performance, Reliable Growth

Our financial results are more than just numbers—they represent a guarantee for you. As a stable, well‑capitalized organization, we ensure the long‑term success of your projects.

  • 2023: Achieved over HUF 1 billion in revenue in our inaugural year, demonstrating the market’s confidence in our capabilities.
  • 2024: Dynamic growth propelled our revenue beyond HUF 4 billion, reinforcing our position at the forefront of solar park construction and grid development.
  • 2025 (H1): Expanded our portfolio with a milestone deployment of one of Hungary’s first 32 MWh energy storage systems in Szentmártonkáta, paving the way for the industry’s future.

International Outlook, Local Expertise

From 2025 onward, we are extending our expertise to the international arena. With strategic projects in Romania, Bulgaria, and Spain, we position ourselves as a key player in the Central European region—bringing the latest global trends and technologies to our domestic clients.

Investment in Quality and Efficiency

We know that project success lies in the details. That’s why we continually invest in:

  • State‑of‑the‑Art Equipment: Over HUF 100 million invested in modern machinery to ensure rapid, precise execution.
  • Dedicated Team: A 60‑strong cadre of highly skilled engineers and specialists committed to delivering your vision at the highest quality standards.

Our Mission

Our mission is to actively shape both the Hungarian and international energy markets through innovative solutions and resource‑efficient operations. We don’t just build solar parks and substations—we deliver safe, efficient, and sustainable energy solutions that create lasting value for our clients and advance the green future.

Certifications

Our company operates integrated management systems built upon the strategically critical pillars of continuous improvement and sustainable operations. Three internationally recognized certifications ensure that our projects consistently meet the most stringent quality, environmental, and occupational health & safety standards:

Quality Management System
