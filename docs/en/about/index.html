Our Story

Founded in 2023, MOVASOL ENERGY Plc. was conceived to translate decades of leadership in the Hungarian energy sector into a dynamic, client-focused enterprise. Our founders, László Módos (ex-Deputy CEO of MAVÍR Plc.) and Gusztáv Varga (former senior executive at MVM OVIT Plc. and KOM Plc.), identified a market demand for a partner that seamlessly integrates deep expertise with agility and innovation. We became that partner.

Proven Performance, Reliable Growth

Our financial accomplishments are more than metrics—they are a tangible assurance of project stability. As a robust, capitalized organization, we safeguard the long-term success of your energy initiatives.

  • 2023: Generated in excess of HUF 1 billion in revenue during our first year, validating stakeholder confidence.
  • 2024: Surpassed HUF 4 billion in revenue, reinforcing our leadership in solar park construction and grid enhancement.
  • 2025 (H1): Deployed one of Hungary’s inaugural 32 MWh energy storage systems in Szentmártonkáta, charting the course for the sector’s future.

International Outlook, Local Expertise

Beginning in 2025, we leveraged our competencies on a cross-border scale. Our projects in Romania, Bulgaria, and Spain position us as a pivotal Central European energy innovator—delivering global best practices directly to our clients.

Investment in Quality and Efficiency

We believe excellence is forged in the details. To that end, we continuously invest in:

  • State-of-the-Art Equipment: Over HUF 100 million deployed in advanced machinery for expedited and accurate project delivery.
  • Dedicated Team: A 60-strong contingent of specialized engineers and technical experts committed to realizing your vision at peak quality.

Our Mission: End-to-End Energy for the Future

Movasol Group was established to deliver comprehensive renewable energy services—from investment to operation—ensuring maximum ROI and seamless project delivery. Our strategy rests on two pillars that drive success and stakeholder value.

Movasol Energy: Development & EPC

We bridge the gap between renewable investors, EPC contractors, and local subcontractors in Hungary and the region. Our vertically integrated model spans project development, construction, and commissioning, backed by executive leadership committed to scalable, robust growth and international expansion.

Movasol Operations: Market-Leading O&M

Our pledge is to become the premier service provider for renewable asset operations. We deliver in-house solutions that guarantee above-market availability and cost efficiency, offering end-to-end commissioning, SCADA integration, pre-commissioning tests, and full operational protocols.

Certifications & Standards

Our integrated management systems are anchored in continuous improvement and sustainable operations. Three globally recognized certifications assure compliance with the highest quality, environmental, and health & safety benchmarks:

ISO 9001:2015
Quality Management System

Our risk-based QMS ensures precise deliverable execution, strict milestone adherence, and ongoing client satisfaction enhancement.

ISO 14001:2015
Environmental Management System

Through proactive environmental risk mitigation, we minimize our ecological impact, champion renewable strategies, and pursue carbon neutrality.

ISO 45001:2018
Occupational Health & Safety Management System

Our advanced OHS framework safeguards personnel, mitigates on-site risks, and underpins accident-free, efficient project execution.
